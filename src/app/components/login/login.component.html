Game Rules

  • The game has 3 difficulty levels: Easy, Medium, Hard.
  • Each difficulty level has 2 sublevels, making a total of 6 levels.
  • Game starts with AI describing a word and you trying to guess it (AI describes → you guess).
  • After you guess, you will describe a new word and the AI will try to guess it (You describe → AI guesses).
  • You and the AI each start with {{ initialTime }} seconds.
  • Time runs only on the active player's turn.
  • Each correct answer gives you back half the time you took to guess it.
  • Hint usage: -2 seconds
  • Change the word : -2 seconds
  • If your time runs out, the game ends immediately.
  • The winner is the one with more time left at the end!

Description Rules (Referee)

  • Your description must give clear clues that help the AI guess the word.
  • Your description must not reveal the secret word or any close form of it (plural/derived forms).
  • Your description must not give spelling or length clues (like “starts with…”, “ends with…”, “has 5 letters”).
  • Your description must not give rhyme clues (like “rhymes with…”).
  • Your description must not use any of the “Avoid these words”.
  • Your description must not be too vague or generic (like “it’s a thing”, “it’s nice”).
  • Your description must not be incorrect or unrelated to the word.
  • Your description must not contain offensive or inappropriate content.
  • If you submit a hint: it must add at least one new concrete detail. You may use at most one avoided word and give at most one spelling/letter/length clue.
